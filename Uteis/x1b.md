You can use \033 instead of \x1b

# Bold
- bold:   `"\x1b[1m ...... \x1b[0m"`
- red:    `"\x1b[1;31m ... \x1b[0m"`
- green:  `"\x1b[1;32m ... \x1b[0m"`
- yellow: `"\x1b[1;33m ... \x1b[0m"`
- blue:   `"\x1b[1;34m ... \x1b[0m"`
- purple: `"\x1b[1;35m ... \x1b[0m"`
- cyan:   `"\x1b[1;36m ... \x1b[0m"`
- gray:   `"\x1b[1;37m ... \x1b[0m"`
